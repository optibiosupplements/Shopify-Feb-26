Patented green chemistry extraction

Most ashwagandha supplements use chemical solvents or alcohol to extract the active compounds. KSM-66® takes a different approach.

Developed over 14 years of R&D by Ixoreal Biomed, KSM-66® is produced using a patented extraction process based on green chemistry principles — no alcohol, no synthetic solvents. The process uses only the root of the plant (not the leaves) and preserves the full spectrum of naturally occurring bioactive compounds.

The result is a "full-spectrum" root extract that mirrors the plant's original chemistry, standardized to contain at least 5% withanolides — the key bioactive compounds that have been the focus of clinical research.
